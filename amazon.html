Junior Analyst

As a Junior Analyst at Amazon with a package of 7 LPA (Lakhs Per Annum), you can expect to be involved in various analytical and data-driven tasks. While specific job responsibilities may vary based on the team or department you are assigned to, here's a general overview of what you might expect in such a role:

1. Data Analysis:
- Collect, analyze, and interpret data to provide insights and support decision-making processes.
- Use tools like Excel, SQL, or proprietary Amazon analytics tools to manipulate and analyze data.

2. Reporting:
- Generate and maintain regular reports to track key performance indicators (KPIs) and other relevant metrics.
- Communicate findings to team members and stakeholders through clear and concise reports.

3. Market Research:
- Conduct market research to gather information about industry trends, competitor activities, and customer behavior.
- Provide recommendations based on research findings.

4. Forecasting and Predictive Analysis:
- Assist in forecasting future trends and making predictions based on historical data.
- Collaborate with other teams to ensure accurate demand planning.

5. Process Improvement:
- Identify opportunities for process improvement and efficiency in business operations.
- Work on projects to implement improvements and optimize workflows.

6. Cross-Functional Collaboration:
- Collaborate with various teams such as finance, operations, and marketing to gather and analyze data relevant to their functions.
- Provide analytical support for cross-functional projects.

7. Problem Solving:
- Analyze and solve business problems using a data-driven approach.
- Propose solutions and recommendations based on analytical findings.

8. Communication:
- Effectively communicate findings and insights to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.
- Present data in a clear and understandable manner.

9. Adherence to Standards:
- Ensure data integrity and adhere to data governance and compliance standards.

10. Learning and Development:
- Stay updated on analytical tools, techniques, and industry best practices.
- Participate in training programs to enhance analytical skills.

As a Junior Analyst, you will likely have the opportunity to work closely with more senior analysts and gain exposure to a variety of projects within Amazon. Adaptability, attention to detail, and a strong analytical mindset are key attributes for success in this role. Additionally, you may have the chance to explore areas of specialization within the broader analytics field as you gain experience.
