Product Manager

As a Product Manager at Tech Mahindra with 7+ years of experience and a package of 22 LPA (Lakhs Per Annum), your role would encompass strategic leadership, product development, and cross-functional collaboration. Here's an overview of the job description for a Product Manager with this level of experience:

1. Product Strategy:
- Develop and articulate a comprehensive product strategy aligned with the company's overall business goals.
- Define the product roadmap and prioritize features based on customer needs, market trends, and business objectives.

2. Market Research:
- Conduct in-depth market research to identify opportunities, assess competition, and understand customer pain points.
- Use market insights to inform product decisions and ensure a competitive edge.

3. Stakeholder Collaboration:
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including development, marketing, sales, and customer support, to ensure alignment and successful product launches.
- Work closely with senior management to communicate product strategy and obtain buy-in.

4. Product Development:
- Lead the end-to-end product development lifecycle, from concept to launch.
- Define product requirements, specifications, and user stories.

5. User Experience (UX) and Design:
- Work with UX/UI teams to create user-friendly and visually appealing products.
- Ensure that the product meets high standards of usability and customer satisfaction.

6. Project Management:
- Manage product development projects, ensuring on-time delivery and adherence to quality standards.
- Use agile methodologies to facilitate efficient project execution.

7. Financial Management:
- Develop and manage budgets related to product development and launch.
- Monitor and report on financial performance against targets.

8. Data-Driven Decision-Making:
- Use data analytics and key performance indicators (KPIs) to make informed decisions.
- Implement data-driven strategies for product improvement and optimization.

9. Product Marketing:
- Collaborate with the marketing team to develop go-to-market strategies and product positioning.
- Contribute to the creation of marketing collateral and sales enablement materials.

10. Customer Engagement:
- Gather customer feedback through surveys, interviews, and other channels.
- Use customer insights to iterate on product features and enhance user experience.

11. Regulatory Compliance:
- Ensure that products comply with relevant regulations and standards.
- Stay informed about changes in regulations that may impact product development.

12. Innovation:
- Drive innovation within the product portfolio by exploring new technologies and trends.
- Foster a culture of continuous improvement and innovation within the product team.

13. Leadership and Mentorship:
- Provide leadership to the product team, offering guidance and mentorship.
- Foster a collaborative and innovative work environment.

This level of experience and responsibility signifies a senior role where you would be expected to bring a wealth of expertise to guide product development, make strategic decisions, and drive business success through effective product management.
